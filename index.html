Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions about our web development, AI automation, and digital solutions services.

What services does Fastam Solutions provide?

We specialize in web development, AI automation solutions (n8n, Zapier, Power Automate), AI calling agents, Notion workspace setups, Canva designs, and Google Sheets Apps Script automation. Our goal is to streamline your business operations through cutting-edge technology and custom solutions.

How long does it take to complete an automation project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple workflow automations typically take 3-5 business days, while complex integrations with multiple platforms may require 2-3 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase and keep you updated throughout the development process.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes! We offer 30 days of free support for all projects, including bug fixes and minor adjustments. We also provide extended support packages and ongoing maintenance plans to ensure your solutions continue running smoothly. Documentation and training are included with every project.

Can you integrate with existing tools and platforms we use?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating various platforms including CRM systems, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Notion, Slack, email platforms, databases, and hundreds of other tools through n8n, Zapier, and Power Automate. We'll analyze your current tech stack and create seamless integrations.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on project scope, complexity, and specific requirements. Each business has unique needs, so we provide custom quotes after understanding your goals. Contact us for a free consultation and personalized quote tailored to your budget and objectives.

What makes Fastam Solutions different from other service providers?

We combine technical expertise with a client-first approach. Our team delivers custom solutions, not generic templates. We focus on understanding your business processes, provide transparent communication, offer comprehensive documentation, and ensure you can maintain and scale your solutions independently.

Do I need technical knowledge to use your automation solutions?

Not at all! We design user-friendly solutions that anyone can operate. Every project includes training sessions and clear documentation. We ensure your team is comfortable using the new systems before project completion. Our goal is to make technology work for you, not complicate your workflow.

Can you help migrate from one automation tool to another?

Yes! We help businesses migrate between platforms (Zapier to n8n, Power Automate to n8n, etc.). We analyze your existing workflows, recreate them in the new platform with improvements, test thoroughly, and ensure a smooth transition with zero downtime for your operations.
